Quiz Instructions:

This is a multiple choice quiz based on the Marvel Cinematic Universe. You have 60 seconds to complete the entire quiz, and for every wrong answer you will lose 10 seconds so be quick! Each right answer is worth 5 points. Once the quiz is finished you can enter your name to save your final score on the high score leaderboard. Good luck!.

The timer will begin as soon as you hit the start button
